Our Services

Basic

This package is all about setting up processes and reporting. We'll help you establish a solid financial foundation for your startup by setting up your financial processes, preparing regular financial reports, and providing you with the insights you need to make informed decisions about your business.

Advanced

This package includes financial modeling, forecasting, and other specialized services. It's suitable for startups that are looking to raise capital, expand their business, or make strategic decisions and need more detailed financial projections. Our team of experts will work with you to create a detailed financial model, prepare accurate forecasts, and provide you with the insights you need to make informed decisions about your future.

Fundraising

This package includes support for fundraising activities such as creating a fundraising deck, preparing financial projections, and coaching the team on how to communicate the startup's financials with investors. We'll work with you to create a compelling story for your startup and help you present your financials in the best light possible to potential investors. We'll also provide ongoing support and advice as you navigate the fundraising process.
